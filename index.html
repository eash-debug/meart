About

Who
I Am

Enjang Ashari, known as N-Jang, is a bedroom musician and producer from Indonesia. He works across genres — Hip-Hop, Drill, Hyperpop, Alternative Rap — whatever the idea calls for.

His music is built in the bedroom, away from the noise. He also produces for other artists, taking each project as its own thing.
